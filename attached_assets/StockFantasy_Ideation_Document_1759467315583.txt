## 1. Concept Overview
The application is a **fantasy stock trading game**, inspired by Dream11‚Äôs contest model but applied to the Indian stock market.  
Users will create **virtual portfolios** (instead of cricket teams), participate in **market sessions** (daily/weekly contests), and compete on **leaderboards** based on their portfolio performance.  
No real money is involved; the platform uses **virtual coins** for entry and rewards, making it both **educational and entertaining**.

---

## 2. Problem Statement
- Young Indians are highly engaged in trading/stock markets but lack **safe environments** to learn.  
- Fantasy sports apps like Dream11 are massively popular but limited to cricket.  
- No mainstream app combines **gamification + stock trading education** in a fun way.

---

## 3. Value Proposition
- **Learn-by-playing**: Users gain stock market knowledge in a risk-free setup.  
- **Gamified contests**: Compete in daily/weekly sessions for leaderboard ranks.  
- **Community-driven**: Friends and private leagues replicate the social thrill of fantasy sports.  
- **Brand potential**: Positioned as the ‚ÄúDream11 for stocks‚Äù with educational compliance.

---

## 4. Target Audience
- Students and young professionals (18‚Äì30) curious about stocks/crypto.  
- Fantasy sports players looking for new formats.  
- Stock beginners who want **practice before real trading**.  
- EdTech partners (colleges, trading courses).

---

## 5. Core Features (MVP)
1. **Authentication** (Email/Google/Firebase)  
2. **Portfolio Creation** ‚Äì users pick a set of stocks (budget limit).  
3. **Market Sessions** ‚Äì daily/weekly contests tied to stock performance.  
4. **Live Leaderboard** ‚Äì ranks by ROI %.  
5. **Wallet (Virtual Coins)** ‚Äì used for contest entry.  
6. **Results & Insights** ‚Äì ROI, rank, ‚Äúyou outperformed X% players‚Äù.  
7. **Notifications** ‚Äì session results, new contests.  

---

## 6. Future Features (Phase 2+)

## 7. Tech Stack
- **Frontend**: Flutter (cross-platform Android/iOS).  
- **Backend**: Firebase / Node.js (for contest management, leaderboards).  
- **Database**: Firestore (scalable, easy for MVP).  
- **APIs**: Twelve Data / Alpha Vantage (stock market prices).  
- **Auth**: Firebase Auth (Google/Email OTP).

- **Engagement**: Use gamification (XP, badges) to retain users.  
- **Competition**: Differentiate by focusing on education + fun.
